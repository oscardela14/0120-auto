The **WINNER** badge in the Anti-Gravity dashboard is automatically assigned to the strategy with the higher **Smart SEO Score**.

This score is calculated based on a weighted algorithm designed to predict content performance.

## üìä Score Calculation Logic (Total: 100 Points)

### 1. Viral Potential (50%)
- **Base Score**: Based on AI analysis of the topic's trendiness and viral probability.
- **Source**: `predictedStats.viralityScore` or dynamic trend analysis.

### 2. Structural Integrity (30%)
- **Title Length**:
  - **Perfect (10~35 chars)**: +15 Points
  - **Too Short (<10)**: +5 Points (Hard to catch attention)
  - **Too Long (>35)**: +5 Points (Low readability)
- **Content Volume**:
  - **Sufficient**: +15 Points
  - **Insufficient**: +5 Points

### 3. Topic Relevance Bonus (20%)
- Analysis of keywords and topic freshness.
- Variable bonus points (0~20) based on specific keyword combinations.

### 4. Strategic Bonus
- **Strategy B (Viral/Challenge)** receives extra points if it includes provocative elements (e.g., '?', '!', or usage of strong hook words).
